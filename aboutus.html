Our Story.

Our Georgian country house hotel is rich in heritage, contemporary in style, and has been named among the best places to stay, dine, and celebrate in the North East. It's home to a collection of beautiful bedrooms - each one different to the next, but all featuring beautiful design, lots of luxuries, and unbeatable views.

Home to a spa, sauna and gym fit for celebrities, we pride ourselves on offering the very best in leisure and fitness to our members and guests alike. Once you've enjoyed a relaxing dip in our olympic size swimming pool, why not sample the latest culinery delights in our Michelin star restaurant?
